Raven

Ravens can imitate birds with the same vocal note, mimicking perfectly the crows.

  • Scientific Name: Corvus corax
  • Average Length: 60 cm / 2 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20 Years
  • Habitat: can thrive in varied climates

The raven is the largest bird of the crow family: it is twice heavier than a common crow at 1.3 kg (3 pounds), being 60 cm (two feet) long, with a wingspan of almost 1 m (3.3 ft). Ravens can live 30 years in the wild and 70 in captivity.
